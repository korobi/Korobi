==================
Punishments
==================

Overview
========

What is this about?
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The articles in this section explain Korobi's punishment system, which helps you effectively moderate your IRC channel.

Who should read this?
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

This section is intended for people who wish to use Korobi's punishment commands in their channels to painlessly deal with problem users and keep the channel running smoothly. This would typically be channel moderators/ops. 

Introduction
============

Korobi includes commands for warning and kicking as well as temporarily and permanently banning users. Many of these commands can be used anonymously to stop users witch-hunting moderators.
