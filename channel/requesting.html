Requesting Korobi¶

Requesting Korobi for your channel couldn’t be easier! Just ask in #korobi on irc.esper.net. See the EsperNet website for details on connecting to the IRC network.

Prerequisites¶

  • You are a channel operator and can prove so.

  • Your channel is in a supported network.
