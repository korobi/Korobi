Korobi Documentation¶

Introduction¶

Welcome to the official documentation for the Korobi project. It aims to be simple and informative, if you find anything that is hard to understand or bad English please feel free to report it in the form of a GitHub issue, on our Documentation project.

What is Korobi?¶

Korobi is a cross-network IRC bot aimed at making channel management easier. It has the functionality to log and track statistics for channels, as well as providing a variety of other useful utilities.

Warning

Korobi is an ongoing project, and may not always be stable.
